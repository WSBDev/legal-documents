# Privacy Policy for WallStreetBets iOS App

**Last Updated:** April 9, 2026

## 1. Introduction

WallStreetBets ("we," "our," or "the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our iOS mobile application.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Information You Provide

- **Email Address:** Used for account creation and magic link authentication. Your email is sent to our authentication provider (Supabase) to generate secure, passwordless login links. Not currently used for marketing communications.
- **Physical Mailing Address:** Submitted voluntarily for promotional item fulfillment. Stored on your device and transmitted to our backend. This information is currently collected but not yet used for fulfillment purposes.
- **Financial Information:** Read-only brokerage connection through SnapTrade. WallStreetBets never sees, receives, or stores your brokerage credentials. SnapTrade handles all credential management independently. No payment card numbers, bank account numbers, or other financial account details are collected by the App.

### 2.2 Automatically Collected Information

- **Device Identifier:** A push notification token is collected solely for delivering push notifications through Apple Push Notification service (APNs). This identifier is not used for tracking, advertising, or analytics.
- **User ID:** A unique identifier generated by our backend system upon account creation. Used for internal record-keeping, audit logging, and linking your data across App features. This is not collected from you directly.

### 2.3 Information We Do NOT Collect

- Phone number
- Full legal name
- Payment card or bank account numbers
- Browsing or product interaction analytics
- Diagnostic or crash data beyond Apple's default crash handler
- User-generated content
- Location data
- Contacts, photos, or other on-device data

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information for:

- **Authentication:** Verifying your identity through secure, passwordless magic link login
- **Service Delivery:** Enabling brokerage connections, address submission, and promotional item eligibility tracking
- **Notifications:** Sending push notifications about your account and promotional eligibility status
- **Promotional Fulfillment:** Processing and shipping member-exclusive promotional items to your submitted mailing address (future use)
- **Security:** Detecting and preventing fraud, abuse, or unauthorized access
- **Compliance:** Meeting legal obligations and enforcing our Terms of Service

## 4. Data Storage and Security

### 4.1 Data Storage

- **Database:** Account data and mailing addresses are stored in Supabase (PostgreSQL database)
- **On-Device Storage:** Certain data, including your mailing address, is cached locally on your device for performance
- **Location:** Data is stored on servers in the United States

### 4.2 Security Measures

We implement reasonable security measures including:

- Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/TLS) for all network communication
- Passwordless authentication via magic links (no passwords stored)
- Access controls and role-based authentication
- Row-level security policies on database tables
- Secure credential isolation for brokerage connections through SnapTrade

No method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security, but we take commercially reasonable steps to protect your information.

## 5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

### 5.1 We Do NOT Sell Your Data

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties.

### 5.2 Third-Party Tracking

The SnapTrade SDK integrated into the App may combine data from your use of the App with other data sources. Under Apple's App Tracking Transparency framework, this constitutes tracking. You will be prompted to grant or deny tracking permission when the App first attempts this activity.

### 5.3 We May Share Information With:

- **Supabase:** Our authentication and database hosting provider, which processes your email address and account data (subject to their privacy policy)
- **SnapTrade:** Our brokerage integration provider, which processes your brokerage connection data (subject to their privacy policy)
- **Apple Push Notification service:** Your device token for delivering push notifications
- **Expo/EAS:** Our app build and distribution platform (subject to their privacy policy)
- **Legal Requirements:** When required by law, subpoena, or legal process
- **Safety:** To protect rights, property, or safety of users or the public

### 5.4 Anonymous Data

We may share aggregated, anonymized data that cannot identify individual users for analytics, research, or reporting purposes.

The App integrates with third-party services, each governed by their own privacy policies:

- **Supabase:** [Supabase Privacy Policy](https://supabase.com/privacy)

We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third parties. We encourage you to review their policies.

## 7. Data Retention

- **Active Users:** We retain your data while your account remains active
- **Account Deletion:** You can delete your account and all associated data at any time from within the App (Settings > Delete Account). Deletion is processed promptly and permanently removes your data from our systems.
- **Inactive Users:** Data may be deleted after 12 months of inactivity
- **Legal Requirements:** Some data may be retained longer if required by law or to resolve disputes

## 8. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we learn that we have collected information from a child under 13, we will delete it promptly. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us at support@wallstreetbets.com.

## 9. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

- **Access:** Request a copy of your personal information
- **Correction:** Request correction of inaccurate information
- **Deletion:** Delete your account and all associated data directly within the App, or request deletion by contacting us
- **Opt-Out:** Disable push notifications through your device settings or within the App
- **Data Portability:** Receive your data in a portable format

To exercise these rights, use the in-app account deletion feature or contact us at support@wallstreetbets.com.

## 10. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

California residents have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act:

- Right to know what personal information is collected
- Right to know if personal information is sold or disclosed (we do not sell your data)
- Right to opt-out of personal information sales (not applicable -- we do not sell data)
- Right to deletion of personal information
- Right to non-discrimination for exercising privacy rights

To exercise your CCPA rights, contact us at support@wallstreetbets.com.

## 11. European Privacy Rights (GDPR)

European users have rights under the General Data Protection Regulation:

- **Legal Basis:** We process data based on consent (account creation, address submission), contract performance (service delivery), and legitimate interests (security, fraud prevention)
- **Data Protection Officer:** Contact information available upon request at support@wallstreetbets.com
- **Right to Complaint:** You may file complaints with your local data protection authority
- **Data Transfer:** Data is transferred to and stored in the United States. We implement appropriate safeguards for international data transfers.

## 12. Changes to This Privacy Policy

- In-app notifications
- Updated "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy

Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
