# Privacy Policy for WSB Earnings

**Last Updated:** January 14, 2025

## 1. Introduction

WSB Earnings ("we," "our," or "the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our Reddit application.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Information You Provide
- **Reddit Account Information:** When you use the App through Reddit, we may access your Reddit username and public profile information as permitted by Reddit's API
- **Preferences:** Companies you choose to follow, notification preferences, and App settings
- **Community Interactions:** Predictions, votes, and other community engagement data

### 2.2 Automatically Collected Information
- **Usage Data:** Pages viewed, features accessed, time spent in the App
- **Device Information:** Browser type, operating system, device identifiers
- **Log Data:** IP addresses, access times, error logs, and performance metrics

### 2.3 Information from Third Parties
- **Reddit:** Profile information and authentication data through Reddit's API
- **WSB API:** Publicly available earnings call data and transcripts
- **Quartr:** Publicly available earnings call transcripts and audio
- **Market Data Providers:** Public stock price and company information

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information for:
- **Service Delivery:** Providing earnings transcripts, predictions, and notifications
- **Personalization:** Remembering your followed companies and preferences
- **Improvements:** Analyzing usage patterns to enhance App features
- **Communication:** Sending notifications about followed companies' earnings calls
- **Security:** Detecting and preventing fraud, abuse, or security issues
- **Compliance:** Meeting legal obligations and enforcing our Terms of Service

## 4. Data Storage and Security

### 4.1 Data Storage
- **Database:** User preferences and App data are stored in Supabase (PostgreSQL database)
- **Caching:** Temporary data may be cached using Reddit's built-in Redis service
- **Location:** Data is stored on servers in the United States

### 4.2 Security Measures
We implement reasonable security measures including:
- Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/TLS)
- Access controls and authentication
- Regular security audits and updates
- API key rotation and secure credential management

However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

## 5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

### 5.1 We Do NOT Sell Your Data
We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties.

### 5.2 We May Share Information With:
- **Reddit:** Through the Devvit platform API as required for App functionality
- **Supabase:** Our database hosting provider (subject to their privacy policy)
- **Service Providers:** Third-party services that help us operate the App
- **Legal Requirements:** When required by law, subpoena, or legal process
- **Safety:** To protect rights, property, or safety of users or the public

### 5.3 Anonymous Data
We may share aggregated, anonymized data that cannot identify individual users for:
- Analytics and research
- Public statistics about App usage
- Community insights and trends

## 6. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

The App may use:
- **Local Storage:** To save preferences and session data in your browser
- **Cookies:** For authentication and user experience (managed by Reddit/Devvit)
- **Analytics:** To track usage patterns and improve the App

You can control cookies through your browser settings, but this may limit App functionality.

## 7. Third-Party Services

The App integrates with third-party services with their own privacy policies:
- **Reddit:** [Reddit Privacy Policy](https://www.reddit.com/policies/privacy-policy)
- **Supabase:** [Supabase Privacy Policy](https://supabase.com/privacy)
- **WSB API:** [Wall Street Bets API Privacy Policy]
- **Quartr:** [Quartr Privacy Policy](https://www.quartr.com/privacy)

We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third parties.

## 8. Data Retention

- **Active Users:** We retain your data while you actively use the App
- **Inactive Users:** Data may be deleted after 12 months of inactivity
- **Deletion Requests:** You can request data deletion at any time (see Section 10)
- **Legal Requirements:** Some data may be retained longer if required by law

## 9. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for users under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If we learn we have collected such information, we will delete it promptly.

## 10. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:
- **Access:** Request a copy of your personal information
- **Correction:** Request correction of inaccurate information
- **Deletion:** Request deletion of your information
- **Opt-Out:** Unsubscribe from notifications and communications
- **Data Portability:** Receive your data in a portable format

To exercise these rights, contact us through the methods in Section 13.

## 11. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

California residents have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act:
- Right to know what personal information is collected
- Right to know if personal information is sold or disclosed
- Right to opt-out of personal information sales (we do not sell data)
- Right to deletion of personal information
- Right to non-discrimination for exercising privacy rights

## 12. European Privacy Rights (GDPR)

European users have rights under the General Data Protection Regulation:
- **Legal Basis:** We process data based on consent, contract, or legitimate interests
- **Data Protection Officer:** Contact information available upon request
- **Right to Complaint:** File complaints with your local data protection authority
- **Data Transfer:** Data may be transferred to the US with appropriate safeguards

- Updated "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy

Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
